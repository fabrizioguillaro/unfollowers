# Unfollowers

Find out who **doesn't follow you back** using your Instagram *following* and *followers* exports.

Everything runs in the browser. Your files are never uploaded anywhere, the comparison happens entirely on your own device.


## How to use it

1. Download your data from Instagram: **Settings → Accounts Center → Your information and permissions → Download your information**, and choose **Followers and following**. Pick **JSON** as the format. You'll get a `following.json` file and a `followers.json` file.
2. Open the html file in your browser, or go to [https://fabrizioguillaro.github.io/unfollowers/unfollowers.en](https://fabrizioguillaro.github.io/unfollowers/unfollowers.en).
3. Drop the `following` file into the left box and the `followers` file into the right box and click the button.
